Demo Scenario: Ravi's Journey

Ravi, a farmer in rural Gujarat, shifts to organic farming. His land's carbon capture is validated by Gujarat Agriculture University, which issues a verifiable credential.

Ravi's DAO-backed project mints NFTs representing his verified ecological contribution. These are sold to a textile factory in Ahmedabad aiming to offset its emissions transparently.

Our Impact Potential

  • Empowers rural communities (especially in Gujarat) to monetize eco-efforts.
  • Real-time verified transparency in carbon markets.
  • Reduces greenwashing risk.
  • Encourages sustainable local practices.
  • Bridges climate action with financial inclusion.
